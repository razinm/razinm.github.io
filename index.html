about razin.mohammad

born in riyadh, he spent most of his life in the capital of saudi arabia. even before he learned to read and write, drawing was his hobby. he tried to emulate the things he sees around him on paper.

discovering photoshop installed on his computer was the turning point in his life. what was once a hobby, became a passion. with the help of a few online courses, he was able to set his footing and master the software. since then he has been working and improving his creative skills.

he completed high school from international indian school, riyadh.
