about razin.mohammad

razin.mohammad is a graphic designer based in riyadh, saudi arabia. with over 5 years of experience, he designs professional looking logos and identity systems for brands that are either new or exisiting ones looking for a change.

born in riyadh, he spent most of his life in the capital of saudi arabia. even before he learned to read and write drawing was his hobby. he tried to emulate the things he sees around him on paper.

discovering photoshop installed on his computer was a turning point in his life. what was once a hobby, became a passion. since then he has been working amd improving his creative skills.

he has finished secondary and primary education from international indian school, riyadh. he is planning to take business and management for higher studies.
